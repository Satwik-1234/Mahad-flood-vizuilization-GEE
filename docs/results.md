## 2. Temporal Evolution
The time-series analysis (refer to the main README chart) reveals:
1.  **Onset**: Early July (Soil saturation).

## 3. Limitations & Uncertainties
* **Urban Bounce**: Double-bounce scattering in built-up areas may mask floodwaters in dense city streets (urban flooding is under-represented).
* **Vegetation**: Emergent crops (paddy) extending *above* the water level may return high backscatter, potentially underestimating the true flood extent.
